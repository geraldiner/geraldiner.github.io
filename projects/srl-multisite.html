Stakeholder Interviews

Interviews revealed unique traits of SRL that would set them apart from competitors, which weren‚Äôt currently being publicized.

In the first week with SRL, my time was split between getting settled and working on this website. As I was meeting up with all my coworkers, I took the opportunity to also learn more about SRL as an organization.

With Dr. Shepherd himself, he laid out what he wanted out of the website and as the only designer on the team, trusted me to figure out how to make that happen.

Building off this, with subject matter experts, such as the Program Manager, Clinical Research Coordinators, Exercise Physiologist, and Graduate Students, I got a better understanding of all the individual components behind SRL. From the Body Composition Lab to past and current research studies to futuristic AI pursuits, I could see what made SRL unique.

Information Architecture

Visualizing the relationships between the various parts of SRL and the bigger picture of their mission helped to build the skeleton for the site.

Ideas from the interviews are implemented into the structure of the site.

The research projects and lab services are the key offerings from SRL. It was important to categorize and organize them in a way that made sense for viewers.

Ultimately, viewers can see and understand the SRL portfolio, and then make decisions about whether or not they align with their own goals.

As a result, SRL is #6 on the first page of Google Search results for exactly that.

Launch

A live minimum viable product (MVP) website gives a realistic experience to encourage useful stakeholder feedback.

Live Shepherd Research Lab website

The different research projects are front and center, featuring the biggest studies and most innovative.

Shape Up! Studies has been running for 5 years and recruitment was at a standstill before establishing the new website.

After additional Facebook Ads, they had 250 website visits in 3 months, with 15 of them leading to recruited participants.

With continuous updates based on feedback, the site attracts industry and student collaborators.
